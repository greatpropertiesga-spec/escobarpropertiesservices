Soft Washing

Soft washing is a low-pressure cleaning method that uses specialized solutions to safely remove organic growth from delicate surfaces. Unlike pressure washing, soft washing treats the root cause of staining, providing longer-lasting results without risk of damage.

Benefits of Soft Washing

Safe for All Surfaces

Low-pressure technique prevents damage to shingles, siding, painted surfaces, and other delicate materials.

Kills Algae at the Root

Our cleaning solutions kill algae, mold, and mildew at the source — not just on the surface — providing results that last much longer.

Extends Surface Life

By eliminating harmful organic growth, soft washing extends the life of your roof, siding, and other surfaces.

Improves Energy Efficiency

A clean roof reflects more sunlight and stays cooler, reducing the load on your air conditioning and lowering energy bills.

Our Step-by-Step Process

  1. Pre-inspection — Assess the surface type and level of contamination.
  2. Solution application — Eco-friendly cleaning solution applied at low pressure.
  3. Dwell time — Solution sits and breaks down organic growth at the root.
  4. Gentle rinse — Light rinsing removes loosened material without surface damage.
  5. Final check — Inspect the surface to ensure complete removal of staining.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between soft washing and pressure washing?+
Soft washing uses low pressure with specialized cleaning solutions to kill and remove organic growth. It is safer for delicate surfaces and provides longer-lasting results.
How long do soft washing results last?+
Typically 2-5 years, depending on the surface and local conditions.
Is soft washing safe for my plants?+
Yes. We take precautions to protect your landscaping and use biodegradable solutions.
