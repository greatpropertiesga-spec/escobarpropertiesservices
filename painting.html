Exterior & Interior Painting

Escobar Property Services delivers professional painting services that transform your home or business. From a fresh coat of exterior paint to a complete interior renovation, our team brings expertise, quality materials, and meticulous attention to detail to every project.

Benefits of Exterior & Interior Painting

Expert Surface Preparation

The secret to a long-lasting paint job is preparation. We clean, sand, caulk, and prime all surfaces before applying any paint.

Premium Quality Materials

We use premium paints and primers specifically selected for durability in Georgia heat and humidity.

Transforms Your Property

A fresh paint job is one of the most cost-effective ways to dramatically improve the appearance and value of your home or business.

Our Step-by-Step Process

  1. Consultation — Discuss your color choices and project scope.
  2. Surface prep — Cleaning, sanding, caulking, and priming all surfaces.
  3. Protection — Masking and covering all areas not being painted.
  4. Paint application — Professional application using proper tools and techniques.
  5. Detail work — Trim, edges, and details receive careful attention.
  6. Final walkthrough — Inspect every surface with you before completion.
