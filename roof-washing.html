Roof Washing

Your roof is one of the most important and expensive parts of your home. Algae, moss, lichen, and dirt not only look bad — they actively damage roofing materials. Escobar Property Services provides professional roof washing using safe, low-pressure soft washing techniques.

Benefits of Roof Washing

Removes Moisture-Trapping Debris

Moss, leaves, and organic matter hold moisture against your roof, leading to premature deterioration. Professional cleaning removes this threat.

Eliminates Algae and Mold

The black streaks on roofs are caused by algae. Left untreated, algae damages shingles and can affect indoor air quality.

Protects from Sun Damage

Clean roofs reflect sunlight better, keeping attic temperatures lower and reducing strain on your cooling system.

Extends Roof Life

Regular professional roof cleaning can add years to your roof life, potentially saving thousands in early replacement costs.

Improves Curb Appeal

A clean roof dramatically improves the appearance of your entire home, increasing property value.

Keeps Your Property Compliant

For commercial properties and HOA communities, a clean roof helps maintain compliance with property standards.

Our Step-by-Step Process

  1. Pre-treatment — Protective measures for landscaping and surroundings.
  2. Debris removal — All loose debris is swept from the roof.
  3. Solution application — Eco-friendly cleaning solution applied via soft wash.
  4. Dwell time — Solution breaks down algae, moss, and staining.
  5. Gentle rinse — Low-pressure rinse removes all residue.
  6. Optional sealant — Water sealant application available to extend clean appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will roof washing damage my shingles?+
Our soft washing method uses low pressure that is safe for all roof types and will not void manufacturer warranties.
How often should roofs be cleaned?+
We recommend every 2-3 years depending on your environment. Properties with heavy tree coverage may need more frequent cleaning.
Can you clean a commercial roof?+
Absolutely. We service commercial and multi-family residential roofs of all types and sizes across metro Atlanta.
