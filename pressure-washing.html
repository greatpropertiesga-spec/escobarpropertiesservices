Pressure Washing

Escobar Property Services provides professional pressure washing for residential and commercial properties throughout metro Atlanta. We remove years of dirt, grime, oil stains, and organic buildup — leaving your surfaces looking like new.

Benefits of Pressure Washing

Removes Built-Up Grime

Pressure washing blasts away years of accumulated dirt, oil, algae, and stains that regular cleaning simply cannot remove.

Improves Curb Appeal

A clean driveway, walkway, or patio dramatically improves the appearance of your home or business.

Prevents Damage

Algae, mold, and mildew break down surface materials over time. Regular pressure washing prevents costly repairs.

Eco-Friendly Process

A well-maintained, clean exterior increases property value and makes it more attractive to buyers or tenants.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I pressure wash my driveway?+
We recommend pressure washing driveways once a year, or more frequently in areas with heavy traffic or tree coverage.
Will pressure washing damage my concrete?+
When done correctly with the right equipment and pressure settings, pressure washing is completely safe for concrete, brick, and most hard surfaces.
Do you use eco-friendly products?+
Yes. We use biodegradable, eco-friendly detergents that are safe for your landscaping, pets, and the environment.
How long does it take?+
A standard driveway takes 1-2 hours. Larger areas may take longer. We provide accurate time estimates during your free quote.
