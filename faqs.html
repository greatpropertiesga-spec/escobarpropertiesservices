Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Escobar Property Services.

FAQ

Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. Escobar Property Services is fully licensed and insured for all services we provide in Georgia. We carry general liability insurance and workers compensation on every job.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve all of metro Atlanta including Lawrenceville, Duluth, Johns Creek, Alpharetta, Cumming, Marietta, Smyrna, Decatur, Stone Mountain, and surrounding areas.
How do I get a free estimate?+
Simply call us at 678-488-2716, fill out our contact form, or email info@escobarps.com. We typically respond within a few hours and can usually schedule an estimate within 1-2 business days.
Do I need to be home during the service?+
Not necessarily for exterior services like pressure washing or soft washing. For interior painting or remodeling, we do require access to the property. We will discuss access needs during your estimate.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion of the job unless other arrangements have been made.
Do you offer any guarantees?+
Yes. We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not happy with the results, we will come back and make it right at no additional cost.
How long does pressure washing take?+
A standard driveway takes about 1-2 hours. A full house exterior with driveway and walkways may take 3-5 hours. We provide time estimates during your free quote.
Is soft washing safe for my roof?+
Absolutely. Soft washing is the recommended method for roof cleaning. It uses low pressure that will not damage shingles or void roof warranties. High-pressure washing can damage roofs, which is why we never use it.
Can you work on commercial properties?+
Yes. We service commercial properties, apartment complexes, HOA communities, and businesses throughout metro Atlanta. We offer retainer contracts for regular maintenance.
How do I prepare for pressure washing?+
Move vehicles out of the work area, close all windows and doors, and let us know about any sensitive plants or outdoor furniture. We will handle the rest.
Do you use eco-friendly products?+
Yes. We use biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your landscaping, pets, and the surrounding environment.
