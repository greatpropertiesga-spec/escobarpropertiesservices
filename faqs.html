Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and process.

Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! All estimates are 100% free and come with no obligation. Simply call us at 678-488-2716 or fill out our online form and we'll assess your project and give you a fair price.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, Escobar Property Services is fully licensed and insured. This protects both you and your property on every job we perform, whether residential or commercial.
What areas do you serve?
We serve metro Atlanta and all surrounding areas including Lawrenceville, Lilburn, Duluth, Norcross, Gwinnett County, DeKalb County, Fulton County, and more. Call us to confirm service in your area.
How is soft washing different from pressure washing?
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to clean hard surfaces like concrete driveways and walkways. Soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialized cleaning solutions — it's ideal for roofs, siding, and painted surfaces where high pressure could cause damage.
How often should I have my gutters cleaned?
We recommend cleaning gutters at least twice a year — once in the spring and once in the fall after the leaves have dropped. If you have many trees near your home, quarterly cleaning may be needed.
How long does a typical pressure washing job take?
A standard driveway typically takes 1-2 hours. A full house washing can take 2-4 hours depending on the size. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your day.
Do I need to be home during the service?
For exterior services like pressure washing and gutter cleaning, you don't need to be home — as long as we have access to the work areas and a water source. For interior services, someone must be present.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the job unless otherwise arranged.
Do you do commercial properties?
Absolutely! We service both residential and commercial properties. Whether it's an office building, retail space, parking lot, or apartment complex, we have the equipment and experience to handle commercial jobs of any size.
